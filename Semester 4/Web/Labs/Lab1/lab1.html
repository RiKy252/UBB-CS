Introduction

Football, also known as soccer, is the world's most popular sport. It is played by two teams of 11 players with the aim of scoring goals.


Basic Rules

  • Each team has 11 players, including a goalkeeper.
  • The game consists of two halves of 45 minutes.
  • Players cannot use their hands, except the goalkeeper.
  • The team with the most goals wins.

Did You Know?
